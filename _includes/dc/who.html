Who: The course is aimed at graduate students and other researchers. You don't need to have any previous knowledge of the tools that will be presented at the workshop. That being said, To get the most out of these lessons, it is best to already be familiar with the basics of Python (MRI) and Matlab (EEG) programming, along the lines of what is taught in the Software Carpentry lessons Plotting and Programming in Python, Programming with Python and Programming with Matlab.
